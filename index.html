Our Projects

Enactus KMV strives to reach new milestones to engender an environment that is more potent and thus aims to create an impact and uplift communities.

With our business models, we help the community in optimal utilization of their skills and resources.

Our projects aim at empowering people in need.

We use business and economic concepts and an entrepreneurial approach to improve the quality of life and standard of living of the diverse communities that we work with.

PROJECT PEHEL

Pehel, an initiative by Enactus KMV, strives to empower underprivileged women by providing them with vocational training and additional income opportunities. Our mission is to unlock the potential of the true architects of our society - women.

PROJECT SAHAAYTA

Project Sahaayta is an initiative by Enactus KMV in a bid to spread and save the dying art of pottery and get our artisans the recognition they deserve.By cutting out the middlemen and establishing a direct market link using both offline and online platforms, the project seeks to empower the Potter's community.

Our Stalls

To promote our initiatives and goods, we have taken the initiative to set up stalls in several colleges. We've run more than ten booths in several universities, including shivaji, vivekanda, jmc, etc., and we've had success selling our goods and raising awareness of our programmes. We have been using the greatest marketing techniques and obtaining positive feedback from our clients. These booths have been entertaining while while providing experience. The use of stalls has greatly benefited enactus. Our first stall was in March at Bharati College in their Holi fest Phagun'22. All the stalls were a great success in increasing the awareness about dying arts of India and the vulnerable communities. The main highlight of these stalls is when people asked us about the artists and enthusiastically stepped up to help them. Our motto for the stalls was to spread stories and smiles. Overall we sold 30 variety of products that impacted over 10 Potter families and 7+ women. Contact us If you seek to reach us near or far , our conatct page will lead you to where we are.
